  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1         quart  mayonnaise
     1/2           cup  olive oil
       2   tablespoons  Louisiana hot sauce
       2   tablespoons  lemon juice
       1    tablespoon  lea & perrins worcestershire
       1    tablespoon  mustard
       2   tablespoons  ketchup

Cook macaroni, drain, and cool.  In a large bowl, mix macaroni, shrimp, eggs, onions, celery, olives, and pickles and toss well. Make dressing out of mayonnaise, olive oil, hot sauce, lemon juice Worcestershire sauce, and mustard, and ketchup.  Pour over other ingredients and mix well.  Refrigerate 1 hour before serving. You may have to make more dressing if the salad takes it up. Serves 30 people for a real picnic or party.
