
Recipe By     : Ella Bailey
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2         pound  butter
   1 2/3          cups  sugar
       2          cups  flour
       5                eggs

Cream sugar and butter until light and fluffy.  Sift flour.  Alternately with eggs add flour and eggs to sugar mixture.  Pour into spout pan that has been greased and floured.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until cake test done.
