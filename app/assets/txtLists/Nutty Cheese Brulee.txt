Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Appetizers                       Cheese/eggs                      
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2        ounces  Brie Cheese -- rind removed
                        OR
   2 3/4        ounces  Camembert Cheese -- rind removed
       2     teaspoons  Strawberry Ice Cream Topping
       1    tablespoon  Broken Pecan OR Walnuts

Place the Brie or Camembert cheese in the center of a nonmetal plate or small shallow baking dish. Spoon the ice cream topping over the cheese. Sprinkle with pecans or walnuts. Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power about 15 seconds or till cheese begins to melt and lose its shape. Serve immediately with flat bread or unsalted crackers and apple or pear slices.
