Serving Size  : 2     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Drinks, Juices, & Smoothies      
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           Cup  Shredded Coconut Meat -- ground to butter
       2          Cups  Water
                        Honey -- to taste

Put all ingredients in a blender and whip unti smooth and creamy. Coconut milk is delicious as a beverage or served over cereal.
