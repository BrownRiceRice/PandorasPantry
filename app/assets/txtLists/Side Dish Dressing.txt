Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           can  english peas -- drained
       1           can  mushrooms
       1           can  waterchestnuts
       2        stalks  celery
       1        medium  onion
       1          dash  accent. seasoning mix
       2          cups  Peperridge frarm dressing
       3        sticks  butter

Saute the celery and onions in 2 sticks butter.  Drain juice off mushrooms and mix with 1 tablespoon flour.  Slice waterchestnuts, mix dressing mix according to directions on back of package using 2 cups dressing.  Line bottom of casserole dish with half of the dressing and pour rest of the ingredients in  and finish off with remaining dressing on top.,  heat in 350 degree oven till good and warm. (HOT)
