  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       6                (6 to 8) apples -- peeled and sliced
     1/4      teaspoon  nutmeg
       1         stick  ( 5 oz.) pastry mix
     1/4      teaspoon  cinnamon
       1           cup  brown sugar

Place apples in greased, 8 inch square or round pan. Combine pastry mix (dry) with sugar and spices. Sprinkle over apples.
Bake at 375 degrees for 25 minutes. Serve with ice cream or whipped topping.
