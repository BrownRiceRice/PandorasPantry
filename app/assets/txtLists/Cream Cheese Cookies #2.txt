Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  butter
       3        ounces  cream cheese
       1           cup  sugar
       1                egg yolk
       1      teaspoon  vanilla
   2 1/2          cups  self-rising flour

Mix  butter, cream cheese and sugar together.  Add egg yolk, vanilla and flour.  Mix well.  Bake on ungreased cookie sheet about 12 minutes till lightly brown on bottom.  Top should remain light in color.  Top with nuts or candied fruit if desired.
