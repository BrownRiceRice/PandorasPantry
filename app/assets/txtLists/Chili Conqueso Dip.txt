
Recipe By     : Chris McElmurry, Beech Island, SC
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  green pepper -- diced
       1  small  clove  garlic -- crushed
       1         small  onion -- chopped
       1    tablespoon  butter
       2        medium  tomatoes -- diced
       1         small  canned chopped green Chile -- drained
       1         pound  Velveeta cheese

Saute the green pepper, garlic and onion in butter. Add diced tomatoes and chili and mix thoroughly.  While this mixture is cooking slowly, grate the cheese and melt in top of a double, boiler.  When melted add the other ingredients to the cheese and blend well.  Serve at once in a chafing dish or preheated bowl, with crisp chips for dipping, or bread sticks.
