  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2           cup  flour
       1           cup  toasted wheat germ
     3/4           cup  shortening
     1/2           cup  sugar
       1                egg
       1      teaspoon  grated orange rind
       1      teaspoon  vanilla
     1/2      teaspoon  salt

Combine all ingredients in mixing bowl.Mix at low speed until well mixed. Chill dough. Roll into 1-inch balls. Roll in wheat germ. Place on cookie sheet and bake at 350 F. for 12 to 15 minutes. Each 2 cookie serving exchange of 1 bread and 1 fat. Calories per 2 cookie serving = 109, cho 12, pro 2, fat 6. Each 2 cookie serving has approximately 3/4 teaspoon of sugar equal to 13 calories of sugar. From ADA Family Cookbook.
