Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1        medium  green bell pepper -- thinly sliced
       1        medium  red bell pepper -- thinly sliced
       1        medium  onion -- thinly sliced
       1    tablespoon  vegetable oil
       1       12 inch  pizza crust
       1  14 ounce jar  Ragz Pizza Quick Sauce
       1           cup  shredded Mozzarella cheese

Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. In a large skillet, sauti peppers and onion
in vegetable oil until tender. Evenly top pizza crust with 1/2 jar Ragz
Pizza Quick Sauce and pepper mixture.

Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 10-12 minutes or until bubbly.
