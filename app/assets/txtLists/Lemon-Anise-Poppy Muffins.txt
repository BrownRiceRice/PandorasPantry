Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   1 1/2          cups  whole wheat flour
       1           cup  all-purpose flour
     1/4           cup  sugar
       2     teaspoons  baking powder
       1      teaspoon  baking soda
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
       2   tablespoons  poppy seeds -- (generous
                        heap)
       1                rind of 1 lemon
       1      teaspoon  lemon extract
       1      teaspoon  anise extract
       1                powdered egg substitute
                        (reconstituted)
       1       6 ounce  white wave soy
                        yogurt
       1           cup  orange juice

Combine dry ingredients through poppy seeds. In separate bowl, mix remaining ingredients. Make a well in dry ingredients, and combine wet mixture. Spritz and flour a bundt muffin pan. Spoon in batter equally. The dough will be stiff, you may have to level each muffin with moistened fingers. Bake in pre-heated oven at 350 F, for approx. 22 to 25 minutes. Cool in pan for 5 minutes, and on rack for another 10.
