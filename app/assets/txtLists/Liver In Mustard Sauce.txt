Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        salt
                        ground red cayenne pepper
       4        slices  liver 1/2 -- 3/4"thick
                        oleo (margarine)
       1           cup  shallots, chopped
       1           cup  dry white wine
       1    tablespoon  english mustard

Salt and pepper liver.  Saute over medium to high heat in the margarine (oleo).  Remove liver and keep warm.  Pour off some of melted oleo.  In what is left, saute shallots until clear or tender. Add white wine.  Bring to boil.  Add mustard and blend into mixture. Stir and simmer for a few minutes. Put liver on platter and pour sauce over it.
