Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  cornmeal -- self rising
     1/3           cup  melted shortening -- Or corn oil
       2                eggs -- beaten
       1           cup  cream corn
     2/3           cup  buttermilk
       1           cup  shredded cheddar cheese
       3                jalapeno pepper -- chopped
       1                onion -- grated

Combine cornmeal, shortening and eggs. Mix well.  Stir in corn, onion and buttermilk, mix well. spoon one half the mixture in a greased 12 inch skillet, sprinkle cheese and chopped peppers over mixture and cover with other half of cornmeal mixture.
Bake at 275 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes. (I bake for 1 hour in my oven)
