Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   1 1/2     teaspoons  salt
       1           cup  yellow cornmeal
       2   tablespoons  butter -- melted
       2   tablespoons  Parmesan cheese

lightly butter and 8x8x1 3/4 inch baking pan.

In heavy 4 quart Dutch oven, bring 2 cups water and the salt to a boil.  Slowly add cornmeal, stirring constantly.  Mixture will become very thick.  Turn heat low, cook cornmeal mixture, uncovered and without stirring about 5 minutes.

Spread evenly in prepared baking pan.  Refrigerate until firm.  About 3 hours.  (Make the night before is better).

To serve:  Brush polenta with melted butter.  Sprinkle top with Parmesan cheese.  Broil 4 inches from heat until hot and golden brown, 4 to 6 minutes.  Cut into 16 squares.
