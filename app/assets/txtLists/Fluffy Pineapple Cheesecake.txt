       2       package  lady fingers (unfilled)
       2       package  cream cheese (8 oz size)
       1  20 ounce can  crushed pineapple
     1/4           cup  sugar
      16         ounce  cool whip

Line bottom and sides of 9 1/2 inch spring form pan with both packages of lady fingers. Put Cream cheese, sugar and 1/2 can pineapple in bowl and mix with electric mixer about 1 1/2 minutes. Add rest of pineapple and mix well. Fold in all of Cool Whip. Pour entire ingredients over lady fingers. Refrigerate 5 or 6 hours before serving. NOTE: This takes just a few minutes to put together and needs no cooking.
