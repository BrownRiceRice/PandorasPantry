Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1         large  orange
       3        slices  pineapple -- diced
       1           cup  button mushrooms -- sliced
       2          inch  cucumber -- diced
     1/4           cup  toasted almonds
       1         ounce  cooked rice
       1                banana -- sliced
       2     teaspoons  lemon juice

Carefully peel the skin and seeds from orange and cut into 1 inch pieces.
Mix the pineapple with the orange, add the mushrooms and cucumber to the fruit.  Cover and keep in a cool place for 24 hours.
Add almonds, rice, banana and lemon juice and mix thoroughly.  Chill the salad before serving.

serves 4 to 6
