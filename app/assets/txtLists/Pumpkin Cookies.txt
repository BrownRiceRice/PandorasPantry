Serving Size  : 36    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Cookies                          Gluten Free                      
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1      teaspoon  Baking soda
       1      teaspoon  Baking powder (GF)
       1      teaspoon  Cinnamon
       1           cup  Rice flour
     3/4           cup  Potato starch flour
     1/2           cup  Shortening
     3/4           cup  White sugar
       1      teaspoon  Vanilla
       1           cup  Pumpkin
     1/2           cup  Nuts

Preheat oven to 350 F. Sift dry ingredients together. Cream shortening and sugar. Add vanilla and pumpkin. Add dry ingredients and nuts.

Beat until smooth. Shape cookies into 1" balls and place on a greased cookie sheet. Press flat with fork. Bake for 9 to 12 minutes.
