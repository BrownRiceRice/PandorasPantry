Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          bags  frozen spinach
       1         pound  bacon
     1/3           cup  vinegar
       1         large  white onions
       1    tablespoon  sugar

Fry bacon till crisp, drain and crumble, set aside.

Reserve enough bacon fat to brown onion, then dump in spinach, vinegar, sugar and crumble bacon.  Bring to a boil.  simmer 30 minutes. Garnish with 2 hard boiled eggs, finely chopped.
