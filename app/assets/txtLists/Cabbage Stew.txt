  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1                cabbage head -- coarsely chopped
       4         large  carrots -- sliced
       3                potatoes -- cubed
       1                onion -- chopped
       5        cloves  garlic
     1/2           cup  white wine -- or veggie broth
     1/2           cup  water -- or veggie broth
     1/2      teaspoon  thyme
     1/2      teaspoon  sage
     1/2      teaspoon  white pepper -- or lemon pepper
                        salt to taste

Put all in large dutch oven, cover and bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer for about 30 minutes, till all is tender. Optional, add mushrooms, zucchini or whatever you like.
