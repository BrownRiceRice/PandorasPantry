Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Soups                            
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2        pounds  stewing chicken
       4          cups  water
   2 1/2     teaspoons  basil
     1/2         pound  onions -- small
       1                bay leaf
       1         clove  garlic
       5                carrots -- sliced
                        parsley and celery leaves
                        ---meatballs---
       1           cup  beef -- finely minced
       1                egg
       1         slice  crumbly white bread
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
                        black pepper -- freshly ground

Place chicken in water in large saucepan.  Add salt, pepper, basil, bay leaf and garlic.  Bring to boil.  Lower heat and simmer slowly for 1/2 hour or until chicken is tender.  Remove chicken from pan.  Cool and carefully skim fat from surface of soup.  Bring soup to a boil and add onions, carrots, parsley and celery, simmer gently for 10 minutes.


MEATBALLS:Mix beef with egg, bread, salt and pepper.  Form into small meatballs, add meatballs to soup and simmer for 35 minutes.  Meanwhile, skim and bone the chicken.  Cut meat into small pieces.  Garnish the soup with chicken and serve.
