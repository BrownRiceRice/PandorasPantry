Serving Size  : 12    Preparation Time : 1:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  butter
       1           cup  sugar
       4                eggs
     1/4           cup  poppy seeds
       2          cups  flour
       1      teaspoon  baking soda
       1           cup  sour cream
       1      teaspoon  almond extract
       1      teaspoon  vanilla

Preheat oven to 350.  Grease and flour 10" Bundt pan.  In large bowl of electric mixer, cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, one at a time.  Add poppyseeds.  Combine dry ingredients and add to creamed mixture, alternately with sour cream.  Add flavorings and turn into baking pan and bake 1 hour.
