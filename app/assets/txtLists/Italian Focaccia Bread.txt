Serving Size  : 15    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Bread                            
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1         pound  frozen bread dough, white or whole-wheat
       2   tablespoons  olive oil
                        HERB TOPPING
       2        cloves  garlic -- minced
       2   tablespoons  fresh rosemary, sage or other herbs
       3   tablespoons  Parmesan cheese
                        OR

                        TOMATO BASIL TOPPING
     1/2           cup  sun-dried tomatoes -- drained and chopped
  2        tablespoons  dried basil
                        OR
  5                     leaves fresh cut basil

Thaw dough until it begins to rise. Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Roll dough into a 9" X 13" rectangle. Cover and let rise 15 minutes.

Punch indentations into dough with fingertips. Brush with oil and cover with topping of your choice.

Bake on ungreased pizza or cookie sheet on lowest oven rack for 15 minutes. Remove to cooling rack and cut into 15 pieces.

Description:
  "A mouth-watering alternative to pizza, focaccia can be served with
  any meal but is especially delicious with Italian dinners."
