  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     3/4           cup  vinegar
     3/4           cup  water
       2   tablespoons  sugar
       1      teaspoon  salt
       4         whole  peppercorns
       4         large  cucumbers
       2          cups  sour cream
                        salt

Combine vinegar, water, sugar, salt and peppercorns.  

Thinly slice cucumbers and marinate in vinegar mixture 3 to 3 hours.  Stir occasionally.. Drain.

Gently mix cucumbers with sour cream and salt to taste

Yield 3 cups.
