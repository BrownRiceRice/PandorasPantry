  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       3          cups  Light Corn Syrup
       3          cups  Sugar
       3          cups  Peanut Butter
       6        quarts  Popped Popcorn (in oil/salt)

Combine corn syrup, sugar and peanut butter in large pan.
Bring to boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.
Reduce heat to low and cook for 3 minutes, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and stir in popcorn.
Toss to coat well. Press into greased pans.
Cool.
Cut each pan into 9 x 6 squares (2" x 2 " squares).
