Serving Size  : 12    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        -----------crust------------
   1 1/2          cups  graham crakers
       2   tablespoons  sugar
       3   tablespoons  butter -- melted
                        ----------filling-----------
      19        ounces  cream cheese; softened
       1           cup  sugar
       2     teaspoons  lemon peel; grated
     1/4      teaspoon  vanilla
       3                eggs
                        -----------glaze------------
       1           cup  mashed strawberries
       1           cup  sugar
       3   tablespoons  cornstarch
     1/3           cup  water

Pre-heat oven to 350F.  CRUST: Stir together graham cracker crumbs and sugar. Mix in butter thoroughly and press into a 9" springform pan. Bake 10 minutes. Cool.  Reduce oven temperature to 300F. FILLING: Beat cream cheese in a large mixing bowl. Gradually add sugar, beating until fluffy. Add lemon peel and vanilla. Beat in eggs one at a time. Pour into shell. Bake 1 hour or until center is firm. Cool to room temperature and then spread with the strawberry glaze. Chill 3 hours.

GLAZE: Blend sugar and cornstarch together in a small saucepan. Stir in water and strawberries. Cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and boils. Boil and stir for 1 minute. Cool thoroughly before spreading over cheesecake.
