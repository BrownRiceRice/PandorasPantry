Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Non-Alcoholic Beverages          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2           cup  sugar
       1      teaspoon  yeast -- scant teaspoon
       2    tablespoon  root beer extract

Put ingredients in a gallon jug with about a quart of very warm water. Stir until ingredients are well mixed. Finish filling the jug with warm water. Let stand six hours (just lay the lid on top, do not screw on). At the end of the six hours, screw lid on and refrigerate.
