  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2                boned -- skinned chicken
                        breast
     1/2           cup  dry white wine
       1                juice of 1 lemon
                        oil
     1/2         pound  sliced mushrooms
                        flour, salt -- pepper

Pound chicken breasts to about 1/4" thickness.  Dredge in flour, salt and pepper.  Fry quickly in oil.  Saute sliced mushrooms in same pan. Add juice of one lemon and the white wine. Boil down to about 1/2, then add back the chicken and heat until chicken is warm. -Sandy-N.C.
