  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1         quart  fresh strawberries -- hulled
       3          cups  cold water
     3/4           cup  lemon juice
     3/4           cup  sugar
       2          cups  club soda -- chilled
                        ice

Up to 1 cup sugar may be used to sweeten lemonade. Garnishes could be more strawberries and/or mint leaves. Place strawberries in blender (save some for garnish) add lemon juice, cold water and sugar; whirl til blended to a texture you prefer. Pour into glasses and add club soda and ice. Can use some ice cubes while blending the ingredients if you like.
