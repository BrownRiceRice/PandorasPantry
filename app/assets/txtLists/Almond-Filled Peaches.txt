  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  sugar
     2/3           cup  slivered almonds
     2/3           cup  coarsely crushed amaretti cookies
       3                egg yolks
       6         large  ripe peaches -- sliced in half and peeled
     1/2           cup  dry white wine
     1/2           cup  water
       2   tablespoons  butter

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. 

In a medium bowl, thoroughly combine 1/2 cup sugar, almonds, crushed cookies, and egg yolks; set aside. 

Place peach halves in buttered 13 W 9-inch baking dish. Evenly spoon nut mixture onto peach halves; set aside. 

In a small saucepan, combine wine, water and 1/2 cup sugar; heat until sugar dissolves. Lightly spoon 1 teaspoon sugar syrup over each peach half; pour remaining liquid around peaches. Dot with butter and bake 40 minutes, basting occasionally. Serve warm or at room temperature.
