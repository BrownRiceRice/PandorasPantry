Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4         large  baking potatoes
       4   tablespoons  margarine or butter
     1/2           cup  sour cream -- (i use non-fat)
       4     teaspoons  onion -- grated
       1      teaspoon  salt
     1/2         pound  crabmeat or imitation crab
                        paprika -- to taste

Bake 4 large baking potatoes. Scoop out insides and set aside. Mix together with elec.hand mixer on low speed for one minute: diet margarine, sour cream substitute, grated onion, and salt. Fold in crabmeat gently with a fork Fill potatoes with mixture and sprinkle liberally with paprika. Reheat in 450 degree oven about 15 minutes.
