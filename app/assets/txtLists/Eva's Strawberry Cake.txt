Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1                white cake mix
       1       package  strawberry gelatin powder
     1/2           cup  warm water
       4                eggs
     3/4           cup  oil
      10        ounces  strawberries
                        Frosting
     1/2           cup  butter
       1         pound  powdered sugar

Dissolve jello in warm water, then add eggs, cake mix, oil and 1/2 of the strawberries. .Bake in 3 layers at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.

Mix butter, powered sugar and the remaining half of strawberries. Frost cake.
