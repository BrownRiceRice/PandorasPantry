
Recipe By     : Virginia Akerley
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  minced onions
       2          cans  baled California pea beans -- 1 lb. 12 oz. each
     1/4           cup  tomato-base barbecue sauce
     1/4           cup  dark corn syrup
       2   tablespoons  Worcestershire sauce
       2     teaspoons  dry mustard
       4                frankfurts

Lightly brown the onion in a little oil.  Combine with rest of the ingredients, except frankfurt, and put in 2 1/2 qt. casserole.  ?Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Saute frankfurt cut in bite size pieces and place on top of beans for the last 10 minutes of baking.
