  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
      10          cups  popped popcorn -- 3.5 oz
                        microwave bag is this amt
       3          cups  bugle-shaped corn snacks
     1/4           cup  margarine or butter
       1      teaspoon  italian seasoning
     1/2      teaspoon  garlic powder
     1/3           cup  parmesan cheese

Mix popcorn and corn snack in large microwavable bowl. Place remaining ingredients except cheese in 1 cup micro-safe measure. Microwave on HIGH about 1 minute or until margarine melts; stir. Pour over popcorn mix. Toss until evenly coated. Microwave, uncovered, 2-4 minutes, stirring every minute, until toasted. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese. Serve warm. Makes 10 cups.
