Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time : 0:00

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
      13         ounce  evaporated milk
       2    tablespoon  sugar replacement
   1 1/2           cup  whole milk
       1    tablespoon  vanilla extract
       3                eggs(well beaten)

Combine evaporated milk and sugar replacement.  Beat well until sugar is dissolved.  Add whole milk and vanilla extract; beat well. Add eggs; beat eggs into milk mixture vigorously. Pour into ice cream maker. Freeze according to manufacturer's directions. 1 serving = 1/2 milk, 1/2 lean meat    Calories = 122
