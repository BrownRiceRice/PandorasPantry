  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          cups  sugar
     1/2           cup  milk
       3   tablespoons  cocoa
       1         stalk  margarine -- cut in thin slices
       3   tablespoons  peanut butter
       2          cups  quick-cooking oats
       1    tablespoon  vanilla

Mix sugar, cocoa, and milk.  add margarine and boil for 2 minutes.  Remove from heat and beat in remaining ingredients.

Lay out on counter new foil, enough to put cookies on.  Drop 1 teaspoon on foil and let stand till its gets hard and dry.

Makes about 60 cookies.
