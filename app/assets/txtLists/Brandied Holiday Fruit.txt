
Recipe By     : Army Times 1978
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
      33        Ounces  canned peaches -- drained
  15 1/2        ounces  pineapple slices -- drained
       4        ounces  maraschino cherries -- drained
       1           cup  sugar
                        California brandy

In a one quart jar pack peach halves, pineapple slices and cherries in layers adding about 1/4 cup sugar with each layer until the fruit reaches the neck of the jar.  Pour in enough brandy to cover, stir and seal tightly.

NOTE:  As fruit is removed, add equal amounts of fruit and sugar to the jar and enough brandy to cover.

Make one quart.
