  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       8        ounces  cream cheese -- softened
     1/2           cup  mayonnaise
       1      teaspoon  grated lemon rind
       2     teaspoons  lemon juice
     1/2      teaspoon  dried dill weed
     1/4      teaspoon  salt

In Medium bowl with mixer at medium speed beat together cream cheese, mayonnaise, lemon rind, lemon juice, dill weed and salt until smooth.  Cover, refrigerate. 
Makes 1 1/3 cups.
