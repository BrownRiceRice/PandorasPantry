Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Healthy Eating                   Soups                            
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2        medium  onion -- chopped
       1         small  acorn squash -- peel
   1 1/2    tablespoon  butter -- (1/2 Stick)
   1 1/2          cups  apple juice
   1 1/2          cups  nonfat vegetarian broth
       1         small  apple -- peel
                        salt and white pepper -- to taste
                        sugar -- to taste
                        sour cream -- for garnish, optional
                        parsley -- chopped for garnish

Saute the onion and squash in the butter for 2 minutes.  Add the apple
juice, chicken stock, apple and seasoning.  Cook until vegetables are tender,
then puree in blender.  Serve warm with sour cream on side.  Garnish with
parsley.  Can be served hot or cold.
