  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method

       4          cups  chablis wine
       1           cup  green creme de menthe
       1    tablespoon  onion powder
       1      teaspoon  dried mint (crushed)
       2   tablespoons  Louisiana hot sauce
       1           cup  soy sauce
       1           cup  water
