  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  granulated sugar
     1/2           cup  water
       1      teaspoon  cinnamon
     1/2      teaspoon  nutmeg
     1/2      teaspoon  cloves
       1         pound  roasted peanuts

Boil sugar, water and spices until syrup threads from a spoon (300 F). Drop 1 pound of peanuts into syrup.

Stir until nuts are coated. Pour out on foil; allow to cool. Store in an airtight container.
