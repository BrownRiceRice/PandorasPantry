  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  Flour
       1           cup  Water
     1/2           cup  Salt
       1    tablespoon  Oil
       2     teaspoons  Cream of tartar
       4         drops  Food coloring

Mix all ingredients in a sauce pan, over low heat. Cook until dough pulls from sides of pan.

Knead by hand until dough is smooth in texture. Store in tightly closed container.
