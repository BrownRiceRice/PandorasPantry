Serving Size  : 12    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4   tablespoons  butter
   1 1/2          cups  firmly packed brown sugar
       1                egg
       1    tablespoon  vanilla
   2 1/2          cups  flour
       1      teaspoon  baking soda
       1      teaspoon  salt
       1           cup  sour  cream
       4          cups  rhubarb cut into 1/2" pieces
     1/2           cup  sugar
     1/2      teaspoon  nutmeg

Cream butter and brown sugar until fluffy.  Beat in egg and vanilla. Sift flour with baking soda and salt. Add to mixture. Fold in sour cream and rhubarb. Spoon into greased 9" X 13" pan. Bake at 350 F for 40 minutes. From The Central Market Cookbook by Phyllis Good.
