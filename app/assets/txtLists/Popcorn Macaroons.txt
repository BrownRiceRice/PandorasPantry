  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method

       3                egg white

ds Salt 1/2 ts Baking powder 1 c  Nuts; toasted - OR- 1 c  Coconut; toasted 1 c  Popcorn; popped - chopped in the blender  Beat egg whites til frothy and add the salt and baking powder. beat until stiff. Fold in the toasted nuts or toasted coconut and the chopped popped corn. Drop by teaspoons onto greased cookie sheets. Bake at 350: for 15 mintues, until lightly browned.
