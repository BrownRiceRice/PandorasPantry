Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Cheese/eggs                      
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       3         large  Hard cooked eggs
     1/2      teaspoon  Prepared mustard
       2   tablespoons  Mayo OR Salad dressing
       1          Dash  Pepper

Cut eggs lengthwise into halves. Slip out yolks and mash with a fork. Mix in remaining ingredients. Fill with yolk mixture, heaping it up lightly.
