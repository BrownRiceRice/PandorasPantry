  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2        ounces  praline liquor
       1    tablespoon  instant coffee powder
       1          dash  salt
     1/4           cup  water
   1 1/4          cups  lt brown sugar
       1           can  sweetened condensed milk
     1/2           cup  chopped pecans
       1      teaspoon  vanilla

Combine liquor, coffee, salt, water, sugar in heavy saucepan. Heat, stirring constantly, to boil. Cook without stirring to 230 deg. on candy therm. Blend syrup slowly into milk. Add pecans and vanilla. Serve over ice cream.
