
Recipe By     : 
Serving Size  : 2     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1                onion, medium -- minced
       1                garlic clove -- minced
     1/4           cup  green pepper -- minced
       1           cup  mushrooms -- thinly sliced
       2   tablespoons  parsley -- minced
     1/4           cup  butter -- melted
     1/4           cup  flour
       1           cup  beef bouillon
       1      teaspoon  salt
     1/2      teaspoon  pepper
       5         drops  hot sauce
       2          cups  shrimp -- peeled & deveined
     1/2           cup  sour cream
                        hot cooked noodles

Saute onion, garlic, green pepper, mushrooms, and parsley until tender; blend in flour, and cook 1 minute. Gradually add bouillon; cook over medium eat, stirring constantly, until thickened. Stir in salt, pepper, and hot sauce, mixing well.  Add shrimp and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in   sour cream until thoroughly heated. Serve over hot noodles.
