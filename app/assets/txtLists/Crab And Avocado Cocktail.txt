Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  crab meat -- cooked
       2                jalapeno chiles -- *
     1/4           cup  lime juice
       2   tablespoons  onion -- chopped
       1         clove  garlic -- finely chopped
                        pepper -- dash of
                        lemon or lime wedges
       2                avocados -- peeled & chopped
     1/4           cup  tomato; chopped -- 1 small
       2   tablespoons  olive or vegetable oil
       2   tablespoons  cilantro; fresh -- snipped
     3/4      teaspoon  salt
   1 1/2          cups  lettuce -- finely chopped

*  Jalapeno Chiles should be seeded and finely chopped. Mix all ingredients except lettuce and lime wedges.  Place 1/4 cup of lettuce on each of 6 serving dishes.  Divide the crabmeat mixture among the dishes. Garnish with lime wedges.
