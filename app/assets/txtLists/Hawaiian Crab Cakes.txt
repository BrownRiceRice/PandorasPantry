Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2   tablespoons  oil
   1 1/2          cups  onion -- finely chopped
       2                garlic cloves -- minced
     3/4           cup  tomatoes -- chopped
   1 1/2     teaspoons  salt
     1/2      teaspoon  pepper
       1         pound  crab meat
     1/4           cup  flaked coconut
       1                egg yolk -- beaten
     1/4         pound  butter

Heat oil in skillet. Saute onions and garlic 5 minutes. Add the tomatoes, salt and pepper and cook over low heat 5 mins. Cool 10 minutes. Mix crab coconut, and egg yolk. Shape into 12 patties for a meal or into smaller cakes or balls for appetizers. Saute in butter until lightly browned on both sides.
