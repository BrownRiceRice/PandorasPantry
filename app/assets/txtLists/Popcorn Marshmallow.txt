Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Snacks                           
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
      26          cups  Popped popcorn
       1           cup  Salted peanuts
     1/2           cup  Baking gumdrops
     1/2           cup  margarine
     1/2           cup  Cooking oil
     500         grams  white marshmallows

In a saucepan melt margarine. Stir constantly while marshmallows melt and mix with melted margarine and oil mixture.

Do not allow this to boil, just melt over low heat.

Mix into popped corn mixture. Press into greased tube pan.
