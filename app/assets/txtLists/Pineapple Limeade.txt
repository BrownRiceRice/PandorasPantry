Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Non-Alcoholic Beverages          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  sugar
       3          cups  pineapple juice
     1/2           cup  lime juice
       1         quart  sparkling water; chilled

Mix all ingredients except sparkling water; refrigerate until chilled. Just before serving, stir in sparkling water.  Serve over ice. Garnish with lime slices, if desired.
