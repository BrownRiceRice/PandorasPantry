  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  cheddar cheese -- grated
       1           cup  monterey jack cheese -- shred
       1           cup  parmesan cheese -- grated
       1           cup  mayonnaise
       1         small  onion -- chopped fine
       1         clove  garlic -- minced
       1           can  artichokes -- chopped
       1       10 inch  round sourdough bread -- hollowed out

Mix all the ingredients together and hollow out a round loaf of French bread. Put the mixture into the bread, put lid back on and bake for 1-1/4 hours at 350:.
