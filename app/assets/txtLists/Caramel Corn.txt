  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       6          cups  popped popcorn
     1/2           cup  almonds slivered -- toasted
     3/4           cup  brown sugar -- packed
     1/2           cup  margarine or butter
       2   tablespoons  light corn syrup
     1/8      teaspoon  salt
     1/4      teaspoon  baking soda

MICROWAVE: Combine popcorn and almonds in large micro safe bowl. In 4 cup micro safe cup, combine brown sugar, margarine, corn syrup and salt. Microwave on HIGH for 2 minutes; stir. Microwave on HIGH for 2-3 more minutes or until mixture comes to a rolling boil. Stir in baking soda; pour over popcorn and almonds. Mix well; microwave on HIGH for 2 minutes. Spread on foil or wax paper to cool.
