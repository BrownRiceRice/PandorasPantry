
Recipe By     : Margie Russell
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4                pork chops -- 1 inch thick
       1                bell pepper -- sliced
       2                onions -- sliced
      12   tablespoons  rice -- uncooked
       1           can  consomme
     1/2           cup  water

Brown , salt and pepper both sides of pork chops.  Place in baking dish.  Put onions and pepper slices on each chop.

Sprinkle 3 tablespoons rice around each chop.  Pour mixture of water and consomme over entire dish.  Bake covered at 325 degrees for 60 minutes or until chops are tender.
