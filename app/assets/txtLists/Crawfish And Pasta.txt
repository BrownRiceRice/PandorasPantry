Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4    tablespoon  butter
       4    tablespoon  flour
       6           cup  crawfish milk*
       2         pound  crawfish tails
     1/2      teaspoon  minced garlic
     1/2           cup  chopped green onions

x  Cayenne pepper x  Salt 3/4 c  Dijon mustard 1 lb Fresh pasta *Soak the tails in milk for 1 hour and strain. Melt butter and add flour. Cook for 5 minutes over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add crawfish milk slowly to make a cream sauce. Cook until thickens. Add the mustard, cayenne, and simmer for 5 minutes. Add crawfish tails and green onions and simmer for 3 more minutes. Add salt to taste and serve over fresh pasta.
