  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2   tablespoons  instant coffee
       2     teaspoons  water -- hot
       1           can  sweetened condensed milk
       4          cups  half & half
     3/4           cup  almonds; chopped -- toasted
     1/3           cup  brickle chips
       2     teaspoons  vanilla
       1      teaspoon  almond extract

In small bowl, dissolve coffee in water. In ice cream freezer container, combine all ingredients; mix well. Freeze according to manufacturer's directions. Makes 2 quarts.





Yield:
  "2 quarts"
