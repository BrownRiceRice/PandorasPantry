Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  butter, or margarine
       1           cup  peanut butter, crunchy
       1           cup  sugar
       1           cup  brown sugar -- packed
       2         large  eggs
       2          cups  flour,all-purpose
       1      teaspoon  baking soda
       6        ounces  chocolate chips,semi-sweet

This recipe was a first place winner for youth category at the Del Mar county fair,1993. The two girls won ribbons in several events. Cream butter and peanut butter together; gradually add both sugars and cream until well blended.  Sift together flour and soda; add to creamed mixture. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop from a teaspoon onto a greased baking sheet.  Slightly flatten cookie dough with back of spoon.  Bake in preheated 325 degree oven for 15 minutes. Jo Anne Merrill
