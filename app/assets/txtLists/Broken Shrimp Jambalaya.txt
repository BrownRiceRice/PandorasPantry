Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Cajun                            Fish/sea                         
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  Peanut oil
       4                Onions -- chopped
       5        cloves  Garlic
       1                Bell pepper -- chopped
       2       bunches  Shallots
     1/4         pound  Smoked sausage
       2     teaspoons  Paprika
                        Salt
                        Red, black, white pepper
   1 1/2        pounds  Broken shrimp (cooked)
       3          cups  Rice -- uncooked
       5          cups  Water

Heat oil, add onions, garlic, bell pepper, shallots, smoked sausage, paprika, salt, and peppers and saute well. Add shrimp pieces, rice and water. Bring to boil, cover, and over very low heat, steam for 20 to 25 minutes. Stir with fork and replace cover.
