  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2   tablespoons  unflavored gelatin
     1/4           cup  cold water
       4          cups  tomato juice
       1                Onion -- chopped
     1/2           cup  celery
       4         whole  cloves
       6                Pepper corns
       1      teaspoon  sugar
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
     1/4           cup  lemon

Boil celery, onion, cloves in tomato juice, drain.

Mix gelatin in the water, add mixture above and chill.
