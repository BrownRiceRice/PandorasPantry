  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4   tablespoons  butter
       8        ounces  cream cheese
       2          cups  confectioners' sugar -- sifted
       1      teaspoon  vanilla
     1/2           cup  coconut -- grated
     1/2           cup  chopped pecans

While at room temperature, blend together butter and cream cheese. Slowly blend in confectioners sugar; add vanilla., coconut and pecans.  Mixing well.  Frost the cake.
