Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       6        slices  bacon
       2   tablespoons  chopped onions
       2   tablespoons  margarine
       2   tablespoons  all-purpose flour
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
     1/8      teaspoon  white pepper
       1           cup  sour cream
       6          ears  fresh corn
       1    tablespoon  chopped parsley

Remove husks and silks from corn, rinse ears. Cut kernels from corn to measure 2 cups.  Fry bacon; drain and crumble.  Set aside.

Saute onion in butter; blend in flour, salt and pepper.  Gradually add sour cream, stirring until mixture is smooth.  Heat just to boiling; add corn, and heat thoroughly (about 5 minutes).  Fold in half of bacon.

Spoon into a greased 1 quart casserole; top with parsley and remaining bacon.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.

Serves 4 to 6
