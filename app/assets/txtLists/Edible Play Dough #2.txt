  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/3           cup  Margarine
     1/3           cup  Light corn syrup
     1/2      teaspoon  Salt
       1      teaspoon  Vanilla extract
       1         pound  Powdered sugar
                        Food coloring -- optional

Mix first 4 ingredients together. Add sugar.

Knead it. Divide and add food coloring. Keep refrigerated to keep from spoiling and when not in use.
