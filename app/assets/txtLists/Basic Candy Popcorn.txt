  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  water
       1           cup  sugar
     3/8           cup  white corn syrup
       1    tablespoon  butter
                        oil of peppermint *
       2         drops  food coloring
     1/2           cup  popcorn -- popped

In a heavy saucepan put the water, sugar, corn syrup and butter. Cook over a moderate heat to 280: on a candy thermometer. Add the oil and the food coloring. Stir well and pour over the popped corn.

 * You can use concentrated flavorings in place of the oil of peppermint
