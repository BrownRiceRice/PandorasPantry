
Recipe By     : Deb Steelman
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 1:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  lentils
     2/3           cup  rice
   2 1/2          cups  water
       1         slice  wheat bread
     1/2           cup  onion -- chopped
     1/4      teaspoon  celery salt

Cook Lentils and rice together in water till water is absorbed. mash lentils and rice.  Tear bread up and add to lentil mixture with the remaining ingredients.  Mix thoroughly, and form into patties. Broil until browned, turning once.
