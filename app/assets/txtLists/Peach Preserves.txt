Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1         large  orange
   1 1/2        quarts  diced peaches
                        juice from 2 lemons
       1           cup  maraschino cherries -- chopped
       6          cups  sugar

Grate orange peel, dice orange fruit.  Combine with peaches lemon juice, cherries and sugar in large saucepan.  cook until desired consistency.

Ladle into sterilized hot jars to within 1/8 inch of jar top.  Wipe jar rim; adjust lids.  process in boiling water bath 5 minutes.  Remove from canner and complete seals. 

Makes 3 1/2 pints.
