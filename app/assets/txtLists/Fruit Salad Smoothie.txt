Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Beverages                        Fruit                            
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1        Medium  Peach -- Ripe
     3/4           Cup  Strawberries -- Fresh OR frozen
     1/2                Banana -- Peeled
       2           Cup  Evaporated milk -- Skimmed and chilled
       4      Teaspoon  Frozen orange juice concentrate
       1      Teaspoon  Vanilla
       4                ice cubes -- (4 to 6)
                        Cinnamon -- Optional

  Combine everything in blender except ice and cinnamon.
  With blender running, add ice cubes one at a time.
  Divide Smoothie into 4 chilled glasses and sprinkle
  with cinnamon.
