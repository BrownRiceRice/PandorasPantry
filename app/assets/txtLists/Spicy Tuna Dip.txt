  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           can  tuna (13-oz)
       1   6 1/2 ounce  or can jalapeno chiles -- chopped
       1                onion -- minced
     1/2           cup  mayonnaise
                        chopped cilantro
                        tortilla chips (optional)

Mix tuna with chopped jalapeno chiles and jalapeno liquid. Add onion to tuna mixture. Stir in mayonnaise until consistency is mushy. Sprinkle cilantro on top and serve with tortilla chips, if desired. Makes 1 1/2 cups
