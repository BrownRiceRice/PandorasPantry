  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1         pound  dark sweet chocolate
       3        ounces  butter or margarine
     1/2           cup  powdered sugar -- sifted
       3                eggs -- separated
     1/4           cup  kahlua
       1      teaspoon  instant coffee
       2          cups  heavy whipping cream

In top of double boiler, melt chocolate & butter over simmering water. Combine sugar, egg yolks, Kahlua & coffee powder. Blend in chocolate mixture. Whip cream until stiff peaks form; fold into Kahlua-chocolate mixture. Beat egg whites until soft peaks form; fold into mixture. Spoon into serving bowl or dessert glassed. Refrigerate 4 hours or overnight.
