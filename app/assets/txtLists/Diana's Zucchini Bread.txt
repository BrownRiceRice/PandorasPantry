  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       3                eggs
       1           cup  salad oil
       2          cups  dark brown sugar
       1         tbsp.  vanilla
       2          cups  grated zucchini
       3          cups  flour
       1          tsp.  salt
       1          tsp.  baking soda
       1           cup  chopped nuts

Beat eggs until frothy. Add oil, sugar and zucchini. Stir in dry ingredients. Fold in vanilla and nuts. Pour into 2 9x5" greased loaf pans. Bake at 350 for 1 hour. Or for muffins, pour into greased muffin pans and bake at 425 for 18 to 23 minutes.
