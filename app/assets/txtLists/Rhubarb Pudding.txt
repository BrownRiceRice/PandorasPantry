Line a buttered baking dish with slices of plain stale cake or bread. Fill with sweetened rhubarb. Cover and bake at 325-F for 30 minutes. Make a meringue of the egg whites by beating until very stiff and adding 4 Tbsp sugar. Remove pudding from oven and cover with the meringue. Return to oven to brown.
