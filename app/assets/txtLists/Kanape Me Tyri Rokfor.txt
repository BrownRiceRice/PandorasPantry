       6   tablespoons  roquefort cheese
       2   tablespoons  sweet butter (or more)
                        sliced white bread and/or>>>
                        assorted crackers.
                        pickle slices
                        capers

Soften the butter and gradually add the cheese to it; cream to make a smooth paste.  Trim the crusts from the bread and cut to desired shapes. Spread the bread (or crackers) with the cheese mixture. Top each canape with a slice of pickle and a caper.
