  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        spalding
       2        pounds  potatoes
       2                onions (or 3)
       4                carrots (or 5)
   1 1/2        pounds  ripe tomatoes (approx.) or
       1           can  whole tomatoes (30 oz.)
       1            bn  celery
      10          cups  water
       1    tablespoon  coarse salt
       1          dash  pepper
       2   tablespoons  shortening or other oil
                        croutons (optional)

Wash, clean, and chop the potatoes, onions, carrots, and celery. Add to the water and boil for 20 min. Add salt and tomatoes (if you are using ripe tomatoes, pierce with fork) and cook for 30 to 45 min. Remove from the heat.  Pass all the vegs. through a strainer or puree machine or blender, then return to the pot. Add a little pepper and stir in the shortening or serve the soup and add oil at the table. Serve with croutons.
