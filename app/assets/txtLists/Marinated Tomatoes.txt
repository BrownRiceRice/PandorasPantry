Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4                tomatoes; large
       1           cup  vegetable oil
     1/4           cup  wine vinegar
     1/4      teaspoon  mustard; dry
       1      teaspoon  salt
     1/4      teaspoon  black pepper
       1                garlic; clove, large, minced
       1    tablespoon  basil; fresh, chopped
       2                thyme; sprigs, fresh,chopped
       1                marjoram; fresh, sprig, chop
       1    tablespoon  scallion; minced

NOTE: Tomatoes should be peeled and sliced.

Place tomato slices in serving bowl.  Combine remaining ingredients and pour over tomatoes.  Toss lightly.  Chill for 1 hour or longer before serving.
