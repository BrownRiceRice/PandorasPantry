
Recipe By     : Sue Kotecki
Serving Size  : 10    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          cups  flour, all-purpose
       2          cups  brown sugar, packed
     1/2           cup  margarine -- not diet
       1           cup  nuts -- chopped
       2     teaspoons  ground cinnamon
       1      teaspoon  baking soda
       1           cup  sour cream
       1      teaspoon  vanilla extract
       1                egg
       2          cups  apples -- peel,core,chop

Blend flour, sugar and margarine until crumbly.  Stir in nuts.  Press 2-3/4 c. crumb mixture into ungreased 9 x 13 pan.  To remaining mixture, add remaining ingredients.  Stir in apples.  Spoon over base.  Bake 25 - 30 minutes.  Cut into squares.  Serve with whipped cream.
