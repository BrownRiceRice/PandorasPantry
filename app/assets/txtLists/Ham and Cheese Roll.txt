  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       8        ounces  cream cheese -- softened
       2          cups  shredded cheddar cheese
       1      teaspoon  grated onions
       1      teaspoon  dry mustard
     1/2      teaspoon  paprika
   2 1/4        ounces  deviled ham
       1    tablespoon  parsley flakes
     1/2           cup  chopped pecans
                        Parsley sprigs

Combine first 7 ingredients, mixing well, chill thoroughly. shape into an 8 inch roll, and coat with pecans.  chill, garnish with parsley and serve with assorted crackers.
