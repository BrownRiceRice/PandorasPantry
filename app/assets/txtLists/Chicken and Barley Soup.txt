  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4          cups  chicken broth
       1           cup  water
       1           cup  onion -- chopped
       1           cup  carrots -- sliced
     1/2           cup  celery -- sliced
   1 1/2        ounces  barley -- uncooked
     1/2      teaspoon  thyme -- crushed dried
     1/4      teaspoon  turmeric -- optional
      12        ounces  chicken -- cubed cooked
       1    tablespoon  parsley

In large saucepan, combine all ingredients except chicken and parsley.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; simmer 30 to 40 minutes or until barley is tender.  Add chicken and parsley.  Cook until thoroughly heated.
