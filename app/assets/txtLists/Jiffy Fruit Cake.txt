  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       9        ounces  mincemeat
     1/4           cup  warm water
       1       package  white cake mix -- prepared as directed
     1/2           cup  english walnuts
                        Powdered sugar glaze -- see recipe

Soften dry mincemeat with warm water.  Prepare cake mix according to package directions, blend in softened mincemeat, stir in walnuts, pour into greased 9" square cake pan.  Bake in moderate oven (350 degrees) for about 40 minutes.  While still warm decorate cake with English Walnuts halves and drizzle with powdered sugar glaze.
