  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  sugar
     1/2           cup  nuts -- chopped
       1    tablespoon  margarine -- melted
       1      teaspoon  cinnamon
   1 1/2          cups  brown sugar -- packed
     1/2           cup  shortening
       1                egg
       2          cups  flour
       1      teaspoon  baking soda
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
       1           cup  dairy sour cream
   1 1/2          cups  rhubarb -- cut into 1/2"

Mix first 4 ingredients until crumbly; set aside.

Cream together brown sugar, shortening and egg. >>  Stir together flour, soda and salt; add to creamed mixture alternately with sour cream. Stir in rhubarb.

 Turn into a greased and floured 9x13-inch pan. Sprinkle with reserved topping.

 Bake at 350 degrees for 45 to 50 minutes, or until it tests done. Cut into squares, and serve warm or cold. Formatted by Elaine Radis BGMB90B; JUNE, 1993
