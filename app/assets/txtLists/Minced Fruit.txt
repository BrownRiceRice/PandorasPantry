Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          cups  prunes
       2          cups  dried apples
       2          cups  raisins
       1           cup  white corn syrup
     1/4      teaspoon  mace
     1/4           cup  vinegar
   7 1/2          cups  water
       1      teaspoon  cinnamon
     1/2      teaspoon  allspice
       1      teaspoon  salt
     1/2      teaspoon  cloves

Wash prunes.  Remove pits.  Chop prunes.  Wash apples. Remove cores. Combine apples and 6 cups water.  Simmer 40 minutes. Combine sirup, vinegar, 1 1/2 cups water, spices, and salt.  Boil 2 minutes. Add prunes. Simmer 10 minutes.  Add apples and raisins. Simmer about 20 minutes, or until flavors are blended. Pack in freshly sterilized jars. Seal.
