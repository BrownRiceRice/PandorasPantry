  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1          pint  nonfat vanilla frozen yogurt
                        OR
       1          pint  nonfat vanilla ice cream
   2 1/2          cups  strawberries
                        OR
   2 1/2          cups  assorted berries
     1/2           cup  nonfat milk
     1/4           cup  honey
       4         small  mint sprigs

Combine all ingredients except mint sprigs in blender or food processor; process about 30 seconds or until smooth. Pour into tall glasses. Garnish with mint sprigs.
