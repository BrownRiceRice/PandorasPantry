Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1       package  frozen french sliced
       3    tablespoon  italian style bread crumbs
                        green beans (9oz)
     1/8      teaspoon  garlic powder
       2    tablespoon  butter
     1/4           cup  parmesan cheese -- grated

Cook green beans according to package. Drain. Toss with butter. Sprinkle with crumbs and garlic powder and toss again. Place in shallow casserole, sprinkle with parmesan cheese and bake, covered, at 350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes, or until cheese melts and beans are hot.
