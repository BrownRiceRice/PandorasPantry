  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2        pounds  beef -- cut in 1-inch cubes
       5                carrots -- sliced
       1         large  onion -- diced
       3        stalks  celery -- sliced
      28        ounces  tomatoes
     1/2           cup  quick-cooking tapioca
       1         whole  clove
       2                bay leaves
                        salt and pepper to taste

Trim all fat from meat.  Put all ingredients in crock-pot.  Mix thoroughly.  Cover and cook on low 10 - 12 hours.  (High; 5 to 6 hours). To enhance the flavor, put in a day of snow skiing.  Then have this for dinner.
