Serving Size  : 10    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  butter
   1 1/2          cups  flour, all-purpose
       1           cup  sugar
       1      teaspoon  baking soda
       2                eggs -- beaten
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
       4                bananas -- finely crushed
     1/2      teaspoon  vanilla

Cream together butter and sugar.  Add eggs and crushed bananas. Combine well.  Sift together flour, soda and salt.  Add to creamed mixture.  Add vanilla.  Pour into greased and floured loaf pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes.  Keeps well, refrigerated.  Yield 1 Loaf. A standard recipe requiring leavening can be made egg-free by increasing the required amount of baking powder by 1 tsp. for each egg omitted.
