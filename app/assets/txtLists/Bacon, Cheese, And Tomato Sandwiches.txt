Serving Size  : 3     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       3        slices  bacon
       3        slices  rye bread -- toasted
       2   tablespoons  mayo. or salad dressing
     1/2      teaspoon  dried dill weed
       1         large  tomato -- sliced
       3        slices  swiss cheese

 Place bacon on microwave rack in glass dish.  Cover loosely and microwave until crisp, 2 1/2 to 3 1/2 minutes.

 Spread toast with mayonnaise; sprinkle with dill.  Place toast slices on serving plate; top with tomato and cheese slices.  Crumble bacon and sprinkle over top.

 Microwave uncovered on high (100%) until cheese begins to melt, 1 to 1 1/2 minutes.
