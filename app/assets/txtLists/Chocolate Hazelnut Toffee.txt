Serving Size  : 24    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Candies                          Dundee Hazelnuts                 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  butter
       1           cup  granulated sugar
       3   tablespoons  water
       1    tablespoon  light corn syrup
     1/3           cup  chopped Oregon hazelnuts
       6       squares  semisweet chocolate (6 to 8 squares)
     2/3           cup  finely chopped Oregon hazelnuts

Melt butter in saucepan. Add sugar, water and corn syrup. Cook to 290: or until a small amount forms a brittle thread in cold water. Remove from heat. Stir in chopped hazelnuts. Spread evenly on buttered baking sheet. Melt chocolate over hot water. Break toffee into small pieces and coat with chocolate, then roll in finely chopped hazelnuts.
