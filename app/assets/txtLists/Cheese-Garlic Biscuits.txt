Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          cups  bisquick. baking mix
     2/3           cup  milk
     1/2           cup  cheddar cheese -- shredded
     1/4           cup  butter or margarine
     1/4      teaspoon  garlic powder

Heat oven to 450 degrees

Mix Bisquik, milk, and cheese until soft dough forms. Beat vigorously 30 seconds. Drop dough by spoonful onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8 to 10 min or until golden brown. Melt and mix margarine and garlic powder. Brush over warm biscuits before removing from cookie sheet Serve warm.

(10-12 biscuits)
