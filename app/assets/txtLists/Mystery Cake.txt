  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     3/4           cup  sugar
       1           cup  flour
       1      teaspoon  baking powder
     1/8      teaspoon  salt
       1        square  bitter chocolate
       2   tablespoons  butter
     1/2           cup  milk
       1      teaspoon  vanilla
     1/2           cup  brown sugar
     1/2           cup  white sugar
       4   tablespoons  cocoa
       1           cup  coffee; strong -- cold

Melt chocolate and butter together add 3/4 c sugar, flour, baking powder and salt and blend well. Combine milk and vanilla. Add and mix well. Pour into greased pan and combine white and brown sugar and cocoa and sprinkle over batter. Pour coffee over top. Bake at 350: for 40 minutes. Serve warm or cold with whipped cream.
