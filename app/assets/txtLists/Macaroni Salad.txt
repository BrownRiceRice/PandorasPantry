Serving Size  : 2     Preparation Time : 0:00

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  cooked elbow macaroni -- chilled
       2    tablespoon  diced pimento
       1                hard cooked egg -- fine chopped
       1    tablespoon  chopped scallion
     1/4           cup  plain low fat yogurt
       1          dash  pepper

In medium mixing bowl,combine all ingredients,mixing well.Cover and refrigerate until flavors blend.At least 1 hour.Makes 2 servings.
