
Recipe By     : Joanne Wakefield
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          cups  corn, cream-style
     1/4      teaspoon  pepper
       1           cup  dry bread crumbs -- med. fine
       1           cup  milk
       2   tablespoons  chopped green bell pepper
       1      teaspoon  salt
       4        ounces  cheddar cheese -- cut in strips
       3        slices  bacon -- cut into 1 1/2" leng

Combine first 6 ingredients.  Pour into 10x6x2" baking dish.  Arrange alternate pieces of bacon and cheese across top.  Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour.
