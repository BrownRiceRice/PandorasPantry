Serving Size  : 3     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Main Dishes                      Nat. Pork Producers Council      
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2        pounds  pork back ribs
       1    tablespoon  creamy peanut butter
       1    tablespoon  curry powder
       1    tablespoon  soy sauce
       1    tablespoon  cooking oil
     1/3           cup  catsup
     1/4           cup  orange juice

In medium bowl, stir together peanut butter, curry powder, soy sauce, oil, catsup and orange juice. Beat until smooth.

Place ribs on shallow baking pan. Brush ribs with glaze and place in a 350:F oven for 1 1/2 hours, brushing with sauce occasionally. Cut into 2-3 rib portions to serve.
