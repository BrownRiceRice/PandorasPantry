Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   1 1/4           cup  fine crumbs(graham cracker
   1 1/2           cup  or dry cereal,zwieback -- etc
       3    tablespoon  margarine (melted)
       1    tablespoon  water
       1                spices (optional)
       1                sugar replacement (optional)

Combine crumbs with melted margarine and water;  add spices and sugar replacement, if desired.  Spread dough evenly in 8-10 inch pie pan, pressing firmly onto sides and bottom. Either chill until set, or bake at 325F for 8-10 minutes.

 1 serving w/ graham cracker = 1 bread, 1 fat, calories: 85 1 serving w/ dry cereal = 1/2 bread, 1 fat calories: 64 1 serving w/ zwieback = 1/2 bread, 1 fat calories: 70
