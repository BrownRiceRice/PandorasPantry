  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1         pound  ground turkey
       1       package  taco seasoning mix
       1           cup  salsa
       1           cup  sour cream
       1           cup  grated Cheddar cheese
     1/2           cup  chopped green onion
     1/4           cup  chopped black olives
                        Taco chips

In a large non-stick skillet, over medium-high heat, sauti turkey 6-8 minutes or until meat is no longer pink. Stir in seasoning mix and remove from heat.
Spread turkey mixture in a 9-inch dish. Sprinkle 1/2 the green onions over turkey and top with salsa. Spread sour cream over salsa and sprinkle cheese over top. Garnish with green onions and olives. Serve with Taco chips.
