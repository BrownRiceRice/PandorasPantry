  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  cream -- whipping
       2   tablespoons  confectioners sugar
     1/2      teaspoon  vanilla
     1/2           cup  marshmallows -- miniature
   1 1/2          cups  rhubarb sauce -- thickened *
       2   tablespoons  almonds; whole -- toasted

* Rhubarb sauce
In a medium bowl with elec mixer, beat cream, sugar, and vanilla until stiff peaks form. Fold in marshmallows In a 1 qt compote dish, layer 1/4 of the cream, 1/3 of Rhubarb sauce, and 1/4 of almonds. Repeat twice. Top with remaining cream and almonds. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
