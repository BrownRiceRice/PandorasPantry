  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4                eggs; separated
   1 3/4          cups  powered sugar; sifted
       1         pinch  salt
       2          cups  flour, all purpose
     1/4      teaspoon  baking powder
       2     teaspoons  aniseed; ground

Grease and flour baking sheets.  Beat egg yolks, powdered sugar and salt in a large bowl until pale and creamy. Beat egg whites until very stiff; fold into egg yolk mixture. Sift flour, baking powder and aniseed onto egg mixture; fold in quickly but thoroughly. Fill a pastry bag fitted with a plain nozzle with cookie mixture. Pipe in small rounds onto prepared baking sheets. Let stand overnight to dry out.  Preheat oven to 325 deg. Bake cookies 20 minutes. Cool on rack.
