  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/3           cup  butter
       2           cup  fresh peach slices
       3    tablespoon  powdered sweetener
       1           cup  flour
     3/4           cup  skim milk
   1 1/2      teaspoon  baking powder

Melt butter in a 1 1/2-qt. baking dish. Add peaches to melted butter. Mix together the sweetener, flour, milk and baking powder. Pour over peaches but DO NOT STIR. Bake at 375 until golden brown.
