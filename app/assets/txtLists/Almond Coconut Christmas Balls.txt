
Recipe By     : Army Times 1978
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   1 1/2          cups  confectioner's sugar
   1 1/2          cups  shredded coconut
       2   tablespoons  cream
     1/3      teaspoon  almond extract
     1/4      teaspoon  melted butter
                        red food coloring
                        green food coloring

Mix together the confectioners' sugar, 1 cup coconut, cream, almond extract and melted butter.  Stir until all are well combined.

Now divide the mixture into three equal parts.  color one pink to red, one green and leave the other one white.  Shape into 3/4 - 1 inch fat balls. Now roll the balls in rest of coconut and let stand about 24 hours to firm up.

Makes 2 to 3 dozen balls.,
