  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2     envelopes  gelatin
     1/2           cup  cold water
       1           cup  chili sauce
       3   tablespoons  lemon juice
       1           cup  mayonnaise
       1           cup  whipping cream -- whipped
       1           cup  ripe olives -- quartered
       1           cup  celery -- finely diced
       2          cans  tuna -- drained

Soften gelatin.  Heat sauce to boiling.  Remove from heat and add softened gelatin and stir until dissolved.  Add lemon juice.  Chill until partly congealed.  Fold in mayonnaise, cream, olives, celery and tuna. Pour into a two quart casserole. Refrigerate until firm.
