Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          cups  fresh cranberries
       1           cup  orange juice
     1/3           cup  sugar
       1         pound  pork sausage
       8        ounces  cornbread stuffing mix -- package mix
     1/2      teaspoon  baking powder
       1           cup  finely chopped celery
     1/2           cup  finely chopped onion
       1                egg
       2   tablespoons  water

In a saucepan, combine cranberries, orange juice and sugar.  bring to a boil for 5 minutes.  Cool.
Break sausage into small pieces over stuffing mix in a large bowl and sprinkle with baking powder.  add celery, onion and egg beaten with water.  Toss gently to mix.
Fold in cooled cooked cranberries.  Spoon mixture into a 2 quart casserole.  Cover tightly and bake in a 325 degree oven for 40 minutes.  Remove cover and continue baking for 15 minutes more.
