Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1         pound  crabmeat
       1           cup  mashed potatoes
       2                eggs, hard boiled -- chopped
       1           sm.  green pepper -- chopped
       1                egg -- beaten
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
                        old bay seasoning
       1          dash  onion powder
       1    tablespoon  parsley -- chopped
                        cracker meal

Saute chopped green pepper and parsley and set aside. Combine crabmeat, mashed potatoes, seasonings, chopped egg, green pepper and parsley, and beaten egg. Shape into croquettes, roll in cracker meal and deep fry until golden brown.
