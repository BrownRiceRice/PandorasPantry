Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Dips                             
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     3/4           cup  Plain Yogurt
       2     teaspoons  Curry powder
       1      teaspoon  Lemon juice
     1/2      teaspoon  Honey
     1/4      teaspoon  Black pepper
     1/8      teaspoon  Hot pepper sauce

In a bowl, combine ingredients, stirring well. Spoon into serving bowl. If possible allow to set for 30 minutes at room temperature before serving. Garnish. Serve with fresh vegetables or pita crisps.

Makes 1 cup
