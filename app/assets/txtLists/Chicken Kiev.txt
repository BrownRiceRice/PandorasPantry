
Recipe By     : The Butcher
Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2                boneless chicken breasts -- halved
     1/4         pound  soft butter
   1 1/2   tablespoons  chopped parsley
     1/2      teaspoon  seasoned pepper
     1/2           cup  flour
                        vegetable oil -- for frying
       1           cup  bread crumbs
       1                egg -- beaten

Make seasoned butter by blending butter, parsley and pepper.  Roll into small rolls and place on wax paper.  Freeze until butter is firm.  After chicken breasts have been boned and halved, take a mallet and pound flat.  Roll the chicken breast around a butter roll, coat each chicken roll in flour and dip in egg.  roll in bread crumbs and fry in deep fat.
