Serving Size  : 0     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Soups                            
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4          cups  light cream
   5 1/2         ounce  chocolate pudding
     1/3           cup  creme de cacao
       2   tablespoons  amaretto
     1/2           cup  toasted almond slivers or coconut shreds

In a medium (3 quart) saucepan, combine 3 cups of the light cream and  the package of chocolate pudding powder.  Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture has come to a full boil.  Remove from heat.  Add the additional cream, Creme de Cacao and Amaretto.  Heat with the chocolate.  

If to be served cold, chill in the refrigerator overnight or for at least 4 hours.  Pour the cold soup into chilled bowls.  Sprinkle toasted almonds or coconut over the surface and serve. To serve hot, present in heated bowls with either of the garnishes.
