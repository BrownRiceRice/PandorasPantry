Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       3                eggs
       1           cup  oil
       1          tbsp  vanilla
       2          cups  sugar
       2          cups  papaya -- puree
     1/4           tsp  baking powder
       2           tsp  baking soda
     1/4           tsp  salt
       1          tbsp  cinnamon
   2 1/2          cups  flour
       1           cup  walnuts

  Beat together, eggs, oil and vanilla. Add sugar and beat. Add papaya puree and blend. Sift dry ingredients together and then blend with creamed mixture. Stir in nuts. Pour into a large bread pan and bake in a 350F oven for about one hour. Remove from pan and place on a cooling rack to cool. This will freeze very well.
