  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1  6 ounce pack  (1 cup) semi-sweet or milk chocolate piece
       3   tablespoons  light corn syrup
       1    tablespoon  water
   5 1/2          cups  raisins

Combine first three ingredients and place over hot (not boiling) water until chocolate melts, stirring frequently.

Remove from heat and stir in raisins, coating well. Drop in small clusters onto waxed paper from teaspoon. Chill until firm.

Source:
  "California Raisin Marketing Association"
S(Internet Address):

Yield:
  "2 1/2 dozen"
