     1/2           cup  bread crumbs
       1    tablespoon  butter -- melted
       1      teaspoon  parmesan cheese -- grated
       1            sm  onion -- chopped
                        vegetable oil
     1/4         pound  fresh mushrooms -- sliced thin
   1 1/2         pound  cream cheese
       4                eggs
     1/2      teaspoon  nutmeg
     1/4           cup  light cream
     1/4           cup  beer
       1         pound  fresh spinach -- washed

Combine bread crumbs, butter & parmesan; press into an 8" springform pan. Bake @350 degrees for 10 min. until lightly browned. Remove from oven; lower oven temp. to 300. In a skillet saute onion until translucent.  Add mushrooms & cook until they "sweat." Cool. Cream cheese, eggs, nutmeg, light cream & beer together until light and fluffy. Steam, drain & chop spinach coarsely.  Squeeze in paper towels to remove excess liquid.  Carefully fold mushroom mixture & spinach into cheese mixture.  Pour into prepared crust & bake for 1 1/2 hours. Turn off oven; set door slightly ajar; and allow cake to cool for 2 hours in oven. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before removing from pan. From "Brew Cuisine"
