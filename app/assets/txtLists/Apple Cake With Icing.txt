  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       3         large  eggs
   1 1/2          cups  oil
       2          cups  sugar
       3          cups  flour
       1      teaspoon  soda
       1      teaspoon  salt
       2     teaspoons  vanilla
       1           cup  pecans
       3          cups  cored, peeled, diced apples
                        FOR ICING:
       1           cup  packed brown sugar
     1/4           cup  milk
       4  ounces (one cube)  margarine

Mix all cake ingredients together and pour into 9 x 13-inch oblong pan. Bake at 350 degrees F. for 45 minutes or until cake tester comes out clean when inserted in center of cake. Cook icing ingredients in small saucepan 2 1/2 minutes and pour over warm cake.
