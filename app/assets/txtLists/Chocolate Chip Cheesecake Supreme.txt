Serving Size  : 10    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  chocolate wafer crumbs
       3   tablespoons  margarine, melted
      24        ounces  cream cheese, softened
     3/4           cup  sugar
     1/4           cup  unbleached all-purpose flour
       3         large  eggs
     1/2           cup  sour cream
       1      teaspoon  vanilla
       1           cup  mini semisweet chips

Combine crumbs and margarine; press onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan. Bake at 350 degrees F., 10 minutes. Combine cream cheese, sugar and flour, mixing at medium speed on electric mixer until well blended.  Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Blend in sour cream and vanilla.  Stir in chocolate chips and pour into crust.  Bake at 350 degrees F., 55 minutes.  Loosen cake from rim of pan; cool before removing rim of pan.  Chill.  Garnish with whipped cream and fresh mint leaves, if desired.
