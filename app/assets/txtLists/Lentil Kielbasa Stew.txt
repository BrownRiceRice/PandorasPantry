  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2                bacon slices -- chopped
       3        medium  onions -- chopped
   1 1/2        quarts  beef stock -- boullion
       2          cups  dried lentils
       1           cup  carrots -- chopped
       1                bay leaf
       1         pinch  thyme
       2         pound  kielbasa -- sliced thinly, divid
                        salt and pepper

Fry bacon and onion in skillet.  Discard drippings (or store in your cherished drippings jar).  Place all ingredients except for 1/2 pound of the kielbasa into a large stockpot.  Bring to a boil and simmer for about 1 hour.  Add remaining keilbasa and cook 10 minutes more.  Season to taste.
