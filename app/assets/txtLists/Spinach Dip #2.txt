  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          cups  mayonnaise
     1/2           cup  parsley sprigs
     1/2         small  onion -- cut up
       1         small  garlic clove
       1    tablespoon  lemon juice
     1/4      teaspoon  pepper
      10        ounces  frozen chopped spinach -- thawed

In covered blender container blend all ingredients at high speed until finely chopped and mixed well.  Cover, refrigerate at least 4 hours.

Makes 2 3/4 cups
