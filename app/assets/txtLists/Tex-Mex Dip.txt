Serving Size  : 12    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Dips                             Mexican                          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2   tablespoons  Lemon juice
     1/2      teaspoon  Salt
       3        medium  Avocados
     1/4      teaspoon  Pepper
       1           cup  Sour cream
     1/2           cup  Mayonnaise
       1                Taco Seasoning Mix packet
      21        ounces  Bean dip -- plain or jalepeno
       1           cup  Green onions -- chopped
       3                Tomatoes -- seeded and chopped
       7        ounces  Olives -- chopped
       8        ounces  Sharp cheddar cheese -- grated
                        Tortilla chips

Peel, pit and mash avocados in medium bowl. Add lemon juice, salt and pepper. In separate bowl, combine sour cream, mayonnaise, and taco seasoning. To assemble, spread bean dip on a large, shallow platter. Top with avocado mixture. Put on sour cream and taco mixture. Sprinkle with chopped onions, tomatoes, and olives. Cover with shredded cheese.
