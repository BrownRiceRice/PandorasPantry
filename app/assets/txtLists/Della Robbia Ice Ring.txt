Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Non-Alcoholic Beverages          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   2 1/2          cups  ginger ale -- chilled
     1/2           cup  lemon juice
                        fruits * -- see directions

*     Fruits should include the following:  Canned apricot halves, drained, Seedless Green Grapes, Strawberries, Maraschino Cherries, Strips of Orange Peel, Curled, and Mint Leaves. Mix ginger ale and lemon juice.  Freeze in decorative mold by putting an inch of the liquid in mold, then adding fruit in layers, more liquid and then freeze.  If fruit tends to float in the liquid, then freeze the mold between each layer of fruit that you add.
