  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  new orleans molasses (dark)
     1/2           cup  brown sugar
     1/2           cup  oil
     1/2      teaspoon  cinnamon
     1/2      teaspoon  cloves
     1/2      teaspoon  nutmeg
       1      teaspoon  ginger
       1           cup  boiling water
   2 1/2          cups  flour -- unsifted
       1      teaspoon  baking soda

Blend together the first 7 ingle. and stir in the boiling water.

 Mix in the unsifted flour. Dissolve the baking soda in 2 T. of hot water; add to batter. Pour into a greased 8x8x2" cake pan. Bake 30 min. at 350F.       Amer. Heart Assoc.
