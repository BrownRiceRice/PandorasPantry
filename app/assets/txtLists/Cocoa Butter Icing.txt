  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           box  Powdered sugar
       1         stick  butter -- softened
     2/3           cup  cocoa
       1      teaspoon  instant coffee
     1/4           cup  boiling water
       1      teaspoon  vanilla

Sift sugar, add cocoa and mix well, add softened butter -  cream well.  Add 1/4 cup boiling water to instant coffee.  Add coffee to creamed mixture and blend thoroughly.  Ice only after cake is totally cold.
