Serving Size  : 12    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
                        -----cake-----
     3/4           cup  butter -- softened
       2          cups  sugar
     3/4           cup  cocoa
       4                egg yolks
       1      teaspoon  baking soda
       2   tablespoons  water -- cold
     1/2           cup  coffee -- cold
     1/2           cup  kahlua
   1 1/3          cups  flour
       2   tablespoons  vanilla
       4                egg whites -- beaten
                        -----glaze-----
       1           cup  powdered sugar
     1/2           cup  kahlua
                        whip cream

Preheat oven to 325:. Grease and flour bundt pan. Cream butter and sugar. Add cocoa and egg yolks. Dissolve soda in water and combine with coffee and kahlua; add to batter alternately with the flour. Add vanilla and fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Pour batter in pan, remove air bubbles. Bake 1 hour. Combine glaze ingredients and pour over warm cake. Cover with foil and freeze. About 1 hour before serving, remove from freezer and ice with whip cream.
