  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1        medium  onion -- chopped
       2   tablespoons  butter
     1/2         pound  ground beef
     3/4      teaspoon  salt
     1/8      teaspoon  pepper
       6          cups  cabbage -- coarsely shredded
  10 1/2        ounces  tomato soup, condensed

Add onion and butter together and heat throughout but not browning.  Spread in 2 quart baking dish.  Add 3 cups shredded cabbage, cover with meat mixture.  Top with 3 more cups of shredded cabbage. Pour over the top the tomato soup.  Bake 1 hour and serve.
