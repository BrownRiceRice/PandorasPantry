  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  sunflower oil
       6        pounds  ground buffalo meat
     1/2           cup  flour
       3          cups  water
     1/4           cup  minced garlic
     3/4           cup  green bell pepper
     3/4           cup  red bell pepper
     1/2           cup  green chili peppers
     3/4           cup  jalapeno peppers
       5          cups  tomato sauce
       3                diced onions
     1/2           cup  beef broth
     1/2           cup  paprika
     1/2           cup  chili powder
       6   tablespoons  ground cumin
       1      teaspoon  cayenne pepper
     1/2      teaspoon  black pepper
     1/2      teaspoon  white pepper
       4     teaspoons  salt
       2   tablespoons  oregano
       2     teaspoons  dried chilies

Saute Buffalo meat, sunflower oil and flour together until meat is cooked. Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 1 hour.
