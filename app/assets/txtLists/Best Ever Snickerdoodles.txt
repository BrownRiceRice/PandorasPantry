Serving Size  : 40    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Cookies and Bars                 Indiana Soybean Board            
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  margarine -- soft
     1/2           cup  soy flour -- sifted
     1/2           cup  all-purpose flour
       1           cup  sugar
       1                egg
     1/2      teaspoon  vanilla
     1/4      teaspoon  baking soda
     1/2      teaspoon  baking powder
     1/2           cup  wheat flour, whole-grain
       2   tablespoons  sugar
       1      teaspoon  cinnamon

Mix margarine on high speed for 30 seconds. Add soy flour, all-purpose flour, 1 cup sugar, egg, vanilla, baking soda and baking powder. Beat together. Beat in the whole wheat flour.

Chill dough for 1 hour. Shape into 1" balls. Roll the balls in sugar/cinnamon mixture and place on a greased cookie sheet. Bake in 375:F oven for 10 minutes. Cool on a wire rack.
