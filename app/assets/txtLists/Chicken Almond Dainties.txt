  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method

                        bread
                        butter -- softened
     1/2           cup  roasted blanched almonds -- slivered
     1/2           cup  chopped celery
       1           cup  cooked diced chicken
       1    tablespoon  pimiento -- optional
       2     teaspoons  lemon juice
                        salt -- to taste
       1          dash  paprika
