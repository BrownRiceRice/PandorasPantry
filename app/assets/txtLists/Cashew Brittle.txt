
Recipe By     : Jo Anne Merrill
Serving Size  : 10    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          cups  sugar
       1           cup  corn syrup, light
       1           cup  butter
     1/2           cup  water
       3          cups  cashews -- chopped
   1 1/2     teaspoons  baking soda -- sifted

  1-Butter 2 large baking sheets; set aside. 2-In a saucepan, combine sugar, corn syrup, butter and water. cook over medium-high heat to boiling; stirring constantly to dissolve sugar. Cook, stirring constantly, to soft crack stage. Stir in chopped cashews and continue cooking, stirring frequently, to hard crack stage. 3-Remove from heat; quickly sprinkle in sifted baking soda over mixture, stirring constantly. Immediately pour mixture onto prepared pans. Cool completely then break candy into pieces. Store tightly covered. This recipe from Rosada Wiseman of San Diego
