Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1         pound  prawns
   1 1/2   tablespoons  fermented black beans
       1         clove  garlic; minced
       1         slice  ginger root
       1         slice  fresh ginger
       1      teaspoon  sugar
       3   tablespoons  oil
     1/2         pound  ground pork -- (not sausage!)
       2     teaspoons  hot bean paste
       2   tablespoons  oyster sauce
     1/4      teaspoon  salt
     2/3           cup  water
       1                egg
       1                scallion -- chopped

Shell, devein and rinse prawns. slice in half lengthwise (butterfly them). Mash bl. bean sauce with garlic, ginger and sugar. Set aside. Add 3 T. oil to wok. Cook pork 2 min. til not pink. Add bl. bean mix. Add prawns and cook 1/2 min on HOT. If you like add pinch of HOT BEAN PASTE (option al); Ad oyster sauce, salt and water. Bring to a boil , cover wok and cook for ONE MINUTE.  Stir in one raw egg, add scallions. Mix a little cornstarch with water.  Add to wok. Stir til gets somewhat thick.
