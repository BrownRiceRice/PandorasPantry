  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1         large  fruit cocktail -- drained
       1                crushed pineapple
       9        ounces  Cool Whip.
       1           box  instant pistachio pudding
       1           cup  coconut
       1           cup  chopped nuts

Drain pineapple and fruit cocktail, mix.  Make pudding and mix with fruit. If desire add coconut and chopped nuts.  Add  cool whip just before serving.
