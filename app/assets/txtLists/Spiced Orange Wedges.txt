  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       4                oranges
       2          cups  sugar
     1/2           cup  vinegar
      12         whole  cloves
       3        pieces  cinnamon stick

Put whole orange in sauce pan.  Add 1 quart water, bring to a boil; boil 20 minutes, drain and cut into eighths.  combine sugar, 1 1/4 cups water, vinegar, cloves and cinnamon sticks.  Stir over low heat until sugar is dissolved.  Bring to a boil, add pieces of orange and simmer abut 20 minutes.  Cool.  cover and store in refrigerator.  Serve as a relish.
makes about 5 cups.
