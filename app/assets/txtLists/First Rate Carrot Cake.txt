
Recipe By     : Jo Anne Merrill
Serving Size  : 12    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          cups  flour, all-purpose
       2     teaspoons  baking soda
       2     teaspoons  cinnamon
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
       3         large  eggs
     3/4           cup  vegetable oil
     3/4           cup  buttermilk
       2          cups  sugar
       2     teaspoons  vanilla extract
       1           can  crushed pineapple -- 8-oz, drained
       2          cups  carrots -- shredded
     1/2           cup  coconut -- dried,shredded
       1           cup  walnuts, black -- chopped
                        cream cheese frosting:
     1/4         pound  butter or margarine
       8        ounces  cream cheese
       1      teaspoon  vanilla extract
       2          cups  powdered sugar -- sifted
       1      teaspoon  orange juice
       1      teaspoon  orange peel -- grated, fresh

Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans or a 13x9x2 inch baking pan. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. For Cake: sift flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt together; set aside. In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs. Add oil, buttermilk, sugar and vanilla; mix well. Add flour mixture, drained pineapple, carrots, coconut and coarsely chopped walnuts. Stir well then pour into pans. Bake for 55 minutes or until toothpick inserted near center comes out clean. Frost with cream cheese frosting; refrigerate cake. Frosting: have butter (or margarine) and cream cheese at room temperature; cream together until fluffy. Add vanilla, powdered sugar, orange juice and peel. Mix  until smooth. This cake was a second place winner at the Del Mar fair. Jo Anne Merrill
