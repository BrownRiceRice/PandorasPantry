  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       5        pounds  canned ham
     1/4           cup  apple juice
       1           cup  cognac
     1/4           cup  corn syrup
     1/4           cup  brown sugar
       1    tablespoon  dry mustard
       1         whole  cloves
                        pineapple -- for decoration
                        maraschino cherries -- for decoration

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Mix apple juice, cognac, corn syrup, brown sugar and mustard together and brush on ham after studding with cloves.  Bake for 1 hour basting frequently with above mixture.
Garnish with pineapple slices and cherries.
