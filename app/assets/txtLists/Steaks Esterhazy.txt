Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4         pound  mushrooms -- diced
       1                carrot; small -- diced
       1                shallot or green onion -- *
       2            tb  butter
       1      teaspoon  paprika
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
       1           cup  sour cream
       1      teaspoon  worcestershire sauce
       4      servings  steak -- **

*    Shallot or Green Onion should be minced **   Steaks should be one of the following:  Sirloin, T-bone, Or Fillet. 

Saute mushrooms, carrot, and shallot or green onion in butter.  Add paprika, salt, sour cream, and Worcestershire sauce.  Simmer for 2 minutes, but DO NOT boil. Broil steaks and top with sauce.
