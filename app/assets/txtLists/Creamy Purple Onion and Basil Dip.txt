Categories    : Cheese/eggs                      Dips                             
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  Cream Cheese -- softened
     1/4           cup  Fresh Basil -- chopped
                        OR
       4     teaspoons  Dried Basil -- crushed
       1           cup  Sour Cream
     1/4           cup  Carrot -- shredded
     1/4      teaspoon  Salt
     1/4           cup  Purple Onion -- chopped
     1/4      teaspoon  Black Pepper

Beat the cream cheese to a smooth consistency, gradually adding the basil. Blend in the sour cream and add the remaining ingredients, blending well. Cover and chill. 

Yield:
  "2 1/2 Cups"
