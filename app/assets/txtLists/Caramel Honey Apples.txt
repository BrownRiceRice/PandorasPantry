Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Candies                          Fruit                            
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  packed brown sugar
     1/2           cup  butter or margarine
     1/2           cup  honey
     1/4           cup  heavy cream
     1/4      teaspoon  ground cinnamon
     1/3           cup  chopped nuts
       6         small  apples with sticks (4 to 6 oz. each)

Combine all ingredients except apples and nuts in 2-quart saucepan. Cook over medium-high heat to 265:F; stir constantly. Remove from heat. Cool 5 minutes. Holding apple by stick, roll in hot honey mixture to coat; roll bottom of apple in nuts if desired. Place on waxed paper squares to cool. Repeat with remaining apples.

Makes 5 to 6 apples.
