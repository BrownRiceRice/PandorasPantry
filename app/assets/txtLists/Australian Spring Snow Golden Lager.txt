Serving Size  : 5     Preparation Time : 1:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       6           lbs  malt extract syrup-light,Australian
       1            oz  Willamette hops -- boiling
     1/2            oz  perle hops -- boiling
     1/2            oz  cascade hops -- finishing
       1           pkg  lager yeast
     3/4             c  corn sugar -- bottling

Boil malt extract and boiling hops with 1.5 gal of water for 60 min.  Add finishing hops during final 1-2 min.  Sparge wort into fermenter with cold water.  Pitch yeast when cool and bottle when done.
