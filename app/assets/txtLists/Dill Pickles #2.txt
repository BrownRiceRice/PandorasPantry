  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1        gallon  cucumbers
       2          cups  vinegar
       2          cups  water
     1/4           cup  salt
     1/2      teaspoon  red pepper -- (cayenne)
       2                Garlic buttons
       2                Dill heads

 Heat vinegar, water and salt to boiling.

 Pour over cucumbers that have been packed in jars with dill, garlic and red pepper.  Seal
