Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  onions -- chopped
       1           cup  celery -- chopped
       2          cups  apples,diced -- unpeeled
       2   tablespoons  butter or margarine
       1           can  cheese soup -- condensed(11oz)
     1/2           cup  milk
       2          cups  rice -- cooked
       2          cans  tuna -- drained(7oz)
       1      teaspoon  salt
     1/4      teaspoon  pepper
     3/4           cup  bread crumbs,buttered -- seasoned

1. Saute onions, celery and apples in butter until tender but not brown. 2. Stir in soup, milk, rice, tuna, salt and pepper; mix thoroughly. 3. Turn into a buttered, shallow 2 1/2-quart casserole; sprinkle with bread crumbs. 4. Bake in preheated 400:F. oven 20 minutes, or until thoroughly heated.
