Serving Size  : 4     Preparation Time : 0:00

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           can  tomatoes
     1/2           cup  parsley -- minced
       1      teaspoon  basil
       1      teaspoon  salt
      10    milliliter  garlic -- minced
     1/4      teaspoon  pepper

To every 3 lbs chicken:  cook in enough oil to brown. Brown chicken in oil and remove from pan; slice an onion and cook in drippings until soft. Then add the above listed ingredients to the pan. Put chicken back in pan and let it simmer for about 40 min uncovered, turning the chicken once in a while.
