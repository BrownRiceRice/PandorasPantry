  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       7           lbs  malt extract syrup-light -- bierkeller (German)
     1/2            lb  crystal malt
     1/2             c  chocolate malt
       2           ozs  hallertauer hops -- boiling
     1/2            oz  hallertauer hops -- finishing
       1           pkg  lager yeast
     3/4             c  corn sugar -- bottling

Add chocolate and crystal malts to 1.5 gal of cold water and bring to a boil.  Remove grains when boiling starts.  Add malt extract and boiling hops and boil 45-60 min.  Add finishing hops during final 2-5 min.  Sparge and pitch when cool.  Bottle when ready.
