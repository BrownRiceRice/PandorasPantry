Serving Size  : 112   Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Cookies                          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2           cup  Butter
       2          cups  Sugar
       2          cups  Brown Sugar
       4                Eggs
       2     teaspoons  Vanilla
       4          cups  Flour
       5          cups  Blended Oatmeal -- (measure and blend
                        to a fine powder)
       1      teaspoon  Salt
       2     teaspoons  Baking Powder
       2     teaspoons  Baking Soda
       4        ounces  Chocolate Chips
       1       8 ounce  Hershey Bar -- grated
       3          cups  Chopped nuts

Cream butter and both sugars. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix together with flour, oatmeal, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Add chips, candy and nuts. Roll into balls and place two (2) inches apart on a cookie sheet. Bake for six (6) minutes at 375 degrees. Makes 112 cookies, but recipe can halved.
