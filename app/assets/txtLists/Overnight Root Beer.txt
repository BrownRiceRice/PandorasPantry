Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Non-Alcoholic Beverages          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   2 1/2           cup  sugar
       3    tablespoon  root beer extract
       1    tablespoon  dry yeast
                        lukewarm water

In a gallon jug with a tight-fitting lid, combine sugar, root beer extract and yeast. Add enough lukewarm water to fill gallon jug. Mix well. Let stand at room temperature for 6 hours. Place in refrigerator overnight or until completely chilled.
