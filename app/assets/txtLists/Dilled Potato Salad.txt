Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/4           cup  mayonnaise -- reduced calorie
     1/4           cup  nonfat yogurt
       1                garlic -- (clove) mashed or
                        tea. garlic powder
       2   tablespoons  dill weed -- fresh and chopped
       1                tea. dried
     1/2      teaspoon  salt free seasoning blend
     1/2      teaspoon  dried mustard
       1         pound  potatoes; red cooked -- cubed
       1           cup  peas -- frozen
       2                green onions -- chopped

In a large salad bowl, combine mayonnaise, yogurt and seasonings. Fold in potatoes, peas and onions.  Cover and chill
