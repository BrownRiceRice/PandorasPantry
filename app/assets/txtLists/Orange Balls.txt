Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
      16        ounces  vanilla wafer crumbs
       6        ounces  orange juice, canned
     1/4           cup  butter
       1           cup  chopped nuts
       1           cup  powdered sugar
       1           box  orange jello

Mix together first four ingredients.  roll into balls.  

Mix together powdered sugar and jello.

Coat balls with sugar and jello mixture by placing in a jar or plastic bag and shaking to coat on all sides.

NOTE:  you can freeze balls before coating with sugar mixture.  When ready to serve thaw and coat with sugar mixture.
