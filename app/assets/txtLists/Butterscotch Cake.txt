
Recipe By     : Jo Anne Merrill
Serving Size  : 10    Preparation Time : 0:35
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2          cups  cake flour -- sifted
       3     teaspoons  baking powder
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
       2        medium  eggs
   1 1/2          cups  brown sugar, packed
     1/2           cup  butter or margarine -- softened
       1      teaspoon  vanilla extract
       1           cup  milk
                        butter frosting:
     1/4           cup  butter or margarine
       2          cups  powdered sugar
     1/2      teaspoon  vanilla extract
       2   tablespoons  cream
       1    tablespoon  hot water

Sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Add brown sugar, butter (or margarine), vanilla and 2/3 cup milk. Beat for 2 minutes at medium speed and then add rest of milk and the eggs. Beat 2 minutes more. Pour into two 8-inch greased and floured cake pans. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 25-30 minutes. Frost with butter frosting. Frosting: Brown butter over low heat, watching carefully. Add rest of ingredients and stir until cool and of consistency to spread. This was a second place winner in the professional category of cake layers at the Del Mar fair, 1993.
