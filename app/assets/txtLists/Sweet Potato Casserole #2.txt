  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       6         large  sweet potatoes
     1/2           cup  butter
                        raisins
       2                oranges -- juice and all
       1          dash  cinnamon
       1           cup  brown sugar
       2                eggs
                        minature marshmellows -- for topping

Ahead of time bake sweet potatoes. Peel and mash.

Add eggs, butter, orange juice, orange sections, cinnamon and brown sugar to mashed potatoes.  Place in a baking dish and bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.  Top with miniature marshmallows and brown.
