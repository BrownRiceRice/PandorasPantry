Serving Size  : 10    Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
      10          cups  popped popcorn
     1/4           cup  parmesan cheese
       2   tablespoons  fresh parsley -- finely chopped
     1/8      teaspoon  garlic powder
     1/2      teaspoon  dried oregano leaves
                        butter flavor non-stick -- cooking spray

Spread popcorn in jelly-roll pan. In small bowl, combine remaining ingredients, except spray. Spray popcorn for 7-8 seconds with cooking spray. Immediately sprinkle with cheese-herb mixture. Toss to combine. Store in tightly covered container.
