  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  sugar
       4                eggs
       4   tablespoons  cornstarch
       1    tablespoon  vanilla
       2   tablespoons  butter
       3          cups  milk

Mix sugar and egg yolks.  Measure out 3 cups milk and use some of it to dissolve cornstarch, then add to milk and butter, mix well.
Beat egg whites and add to milk mixture. 
Pour into pan and bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes.
