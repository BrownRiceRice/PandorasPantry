  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           box  lime jello
       1           cup  boiling water
       3   tablespoons  grated carrots
       3   tablespoons  chopped green bell pepper
       1    tablespoon  grated onion
       1           cup  cream -- whipped
       1           cup  mayonnaise
       1           cup  cottage cheese

Dissolve jello in water,  chill and whip.  Add carrots, peppers and onions.  Whip cream, add mayonnaise and cottage cheese. Mix all together and pour into mold.  Chill until firm.
