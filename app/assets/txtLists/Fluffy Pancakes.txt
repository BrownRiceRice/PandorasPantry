  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   2 1/2          cups  flour
       1      teaspoon  salt
       1      teaspoon  baking soda
       1    tablespoon  brown sugar
       2                eggs
   2 1/2           cup  buttermilk - up to 3 cups
       2    tablespoon  oil

Mix together dry ingredients.

Beat 2 eggs, add 2 1/2 to 3 cup buttermilk (to the eggs) and 2 tablespoons oil

Make a whole in the middle of the dry ingredients and pour the wet in. Try to do as little stirring and possible.  It should be lumpy.
