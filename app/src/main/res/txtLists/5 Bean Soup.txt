Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Soups                            
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           cup  pinto beans
       1           cup  navy beans
       1           cup  black eyed peas
      10           cup  water
       1           cup  chicken bouillon
       1           cup  onion -- diced
       1           can  tomatoes -- cut (16 ounce)
     3/4      teaspoon  thyme
     1/4      teaspoon  pepper
       1           cup  red beans
       1           cup  northern beans
       2         whole  bay leaves
       2         pound  meaty ham bones
   1 1/2           cup  smoked ham
       1    tablespoon  chili powder
     1/4      teaspoon  worcestershire sauce
       1        medium  carrot -- grated

Place beans into 4 quart kettle, cover with 2 inches of water and allow to soak overnight. Drain water then add ham bones, water and bay leaves. Simmer covered for 2 hours.  

Remove ham bones and return any meat to kettle. Add bouillon, onions, chili and garlic powder, thyme, pepper, tomatoes,  carrot and worcestershire sauce. Cover and simmer for 1 hour.  Add water as needed.
