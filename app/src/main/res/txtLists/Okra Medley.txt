  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method

       5        slices  bacon -- cut in slices
       2          cups  sliced okra
       2        medium  tomatoes -- chopped
       1           cup  fresh corn
     1/2           cup  onion
     1/4           cup  celery
