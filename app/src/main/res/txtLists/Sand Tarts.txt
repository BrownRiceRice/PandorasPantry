
Recipe By     : Joyce Ware
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1                egg -- beaten
     1/4           cup  sugar
     1/2           cup  ground pecans
     1/4           cup  margarine
   2 1/4          cups  sifted flour
                        pecan halves -- for topping

Combine egg, sugar and ground pecans. 
Work  margarine with a spoon until fluffy and creamy, then combine with the nut mixture and mix well.  Add flour and mix thoroughly.  Press through cookie press onto greased cookie sheet.,  place a pecan half on top of each cookie.  Bake at 375 degrees for 15 minutes.

Makes about 64 cookies.
