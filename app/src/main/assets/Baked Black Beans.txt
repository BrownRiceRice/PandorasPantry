Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Side Dish                        
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       6                sun-dried tomatoes
                        Vegetable cooking spray
       1           cup  chopped onion
       4        cloves  garlic -- minced
     1/2                (1/2 to 1) jalapeno pepper -- seeds and veins disc
       2  15 ounce can  black beans -- drained and rinsed
       2          cups  frozen thawed whole kernel corn
       1    tablespoon  (1 to 2) honey
       1           cup  finely chopped cilantro
     3/4      teaspoon  dried tarragon leaves
       1                bay leaf
     1/2           cup  (2 ounces) fat-free Cheddar cheese
     1/2           cup  (2 ounces) crumbled Mexican white or farme

Pour hot water over sun-dried tomatoes in small bowl, let stand until tomatoes are soft, about 10 minutes. Drain tomatoes and coarsely chop.

Spray large saucepan with cooking spray; heat over medium heat until hot. Saute onion, garlic and jalapeqo pepper until tender, about 5 minutes.

Combine all ingredients, except cheeses, in 1 1/2-quart casserole; sprinkle cheeses over top. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees F until bean mixture is hot, about 30 minutes or microwave on medium high for 20 minutes. Discard bay leaf.
