Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     1/2           cup  water
     1/4           cup  vinegar
       2   tablespoons  dry mustard
       3   tablespoons  brown sugar
       4   tablespoons  chili sauce
       8                pork chops

Combine ingredients and mix well.  Pour over seasoned chops in glass baking dish, cover and bake at 400 F degrees for 1-1/2 hours, uncovering last 30 minutes.  Water may be added to make gravy. Delicious with ribs also. From "Talk About Good" contributed by Susan Chandler Castille
