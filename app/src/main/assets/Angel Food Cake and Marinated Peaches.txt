  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       3          cups  peeled, sliced fresh peaches
     1/8      teaspoon  ground nutmeg
     1/8      teaspoon  ground cinnamon
     1/4           cup  peach schnapps
       1                angel food cake

Combine first 4 ingredients in a bowl; toss lightly. Cover and marinate in refrigerator 30 minutes to 4 hours. Serve over angel food cake. 

Serving Size: 1/2 cup peach mixture and 1 slice cake
