
Recipe By     : Dot Oellerich
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2        sticks  butter -- melted
       1         pound  chopped dates
       1           can  coconut
       1           cup  nuts
       2          cups  brown sugar
       4          cups  rice krispies.
                        powdered sugar

Melt butter and dates, coconut, nuts and brown sugar.  Cook for 6 minutes and then add rice krispies.  roll in balls and then in powdered sugar. 

Yield approximately 104 balls.
