  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2                eggs -- slightly beaten
       2          cups  solid pack pumpkin
     3/4           cup  sugar
     1/2      teaspoon  salt
       1      teaspoon  cinnamon
     1/2      teaspoon  ginger
     1/4      teaspoon  cloves
   1 2/3          cups  evaporated milk -- or light cream
       1        9 inch  pie shell -- unbaked

Mix ingredients in order given.  Pour into pie shell.  Bake in 425 degree oven for 15 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees; continue to bake for 45 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
