  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           can  peach halves
       2          cans  pears
       1           can  pineapple slices
     1/4         pound  stewed prunes
       4   tablespoons  vinegar
       1   small   can  tomato sauce
       4   tablespoons  butter
     1/2           cup  brown sugar

Drain all can fruits.
Bring tomato sauce, butter, vinegar and brown sugar to a boil.  Remove from heat.  Add fruit and simmer a few minutes till fruit has been heated.  

Great for meats or an accomplishment for buffers.
