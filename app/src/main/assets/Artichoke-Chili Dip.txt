
Recipe By     : Mary Bergeron, Seattle, Wash.
Serving Size  : 1     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
      14        ounces  canned artichoke hearts -- drained and chopped
       4        ounces  canned green chili peppers -- rinsed, seeded, chop
       1           cup  grated Parmesan cheese
       1           cup  mayonnaise
                        Tortilla chips
                        Breadsticks

Combine the chopped artichoke hearts, chopped chili peppers, cheese, and mayonnaise.  Turn mixture into an 8-inch round baking dish.  Bake in a 350 degree oven about 20 minutes or till heated through.  Serve warm with tortilla chips and breadsticks.  Makes about 2 3/4 cups.
