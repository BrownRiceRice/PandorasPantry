  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1           Cup  Almond Butter
       4   Tablespoons  Chopped Onion
       1    Tablespoon  Honey
       1                Lemon -- juiced
       1    Tablespoon  Grated Ginger Root
       1    Tablespoon  Tamari Soy Sauce
       1    Tablespoon  Sesame Oil
     1/2      Teaspoon  Cayenne Pepper
     1/2           Cup  Water

Place all ingredients in blender, add enough water to allow blender to run. Blend until thoroughly pureed. It should the consistency of a smooth, thick soup. Taste and adjust seasonings. 

Your first taste of raw almond butter may seem a bit disappointing - its flavor is more subtle than peanut butter. However, you will find that your palate quickly gets used to it
