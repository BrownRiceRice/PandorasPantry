Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       3          cups  flour
   3 3/4     teaspoons  baking powder
   2 1/4     teaspoons  salt
       1    tablespoon  honey
      12        ounces  beer

Grease 9x5x3" loaf pan. Mix flour, salt, baking power; combine with beer and honey in large bowl. Stir together until well mixed.  Spread batter in prepared pan.  Bake at 350f for 45 min or until browned and a wooden pick comes out clean.  Turn out on cooling rack.  Cool completely before slicing.
