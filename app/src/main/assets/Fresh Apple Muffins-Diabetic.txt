Serving Size  : 12    Preparation Time : 0:00

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       2    tablespoon  soft margarine
       2    tablespoon  sugar replacement
                        egg (beaten)
   1 1/4           cup  flour
     1/4      teaspoon  salt
       2      teaspoon  baking powder
       6    tablespoon  skim milk
                        apple (peeled and chopped)

Cream margarine and sugar replacement;  add egg.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  Spoon into greased muffin tins, filling no more than two-thirds full.  Bake at 400F for 25 minutes, or until done. For those who like spices, you may add cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, cloves to the mix - just a dash of each. yield : 12 muffins 1 muffin = 1 bread calories = 72
