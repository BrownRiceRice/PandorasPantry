Serving Size  : 8     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Non-Alcoholic Beverages          
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1       package  Kool-aid -- grape
       1       package  Kool-aid -- orange
       2          cups  sugar
       3        quarts  water
       1         liter  ginger ale -- cold

Use the unsweetened Kool-Aid In a large non-metal pitcher, combine envelopes of Kool-Aid with sugar and water.  Stir until thoroughly mixed and Kool-Aid is dissolved.  Add ginger ale and serve immediately.  Makes about 8-10 servings.
