  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
      10          cups  all-purpose flour
   2 1/2          cups  nonfat dry milk
     1/2           cup  granulated sugar
     1/4           cup  baking powder
       2   tablespoons  salt

Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl. Stir together to blend well.

Place in a large container until ready to use or place in individual storage bags (1 1/2 cups in each bag).  Store in cool, dry place for up to 8 months.

Makes 13 cups of dry mix.
