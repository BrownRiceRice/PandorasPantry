Serving Size  : 6     Preparation Time : 0:00
Categories    : Cakes                            Fruits                           
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
       1    tablespoon  Butter -- softened
       1           cup  Sugar
   2 1/2          cups  Flour
       3     teaspoons  Baking Powder
   1 1/2          cups  Milk
     3/4           cup  Sugar
                        Mixed with
       2     teaspoons  Cinnamon
       5         large  Peaches -- peeled and sliced
       2   tablespoons  Butter -- melted

Preheat oven to 350. 
With electric mixer, blend first five ingredients. Spread in greased and floured 15x10" jelly roll pan, or 13x9" plus one 8" cake pan. Sprinkle half of cinnamon/sugar mixture lightly over dough base. Arrange peach slices by rows on top of base. Sprinkle with remainder of cinnamon/sugar mixture and drizzle melter butter over all. Bake for 25-30 minutes. (For thicker dough, omit 8" cake pan - use 13x9" pan only.)
